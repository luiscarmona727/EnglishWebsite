Counter-Strike 2 is a multiplayer tactical shooter where two teams, Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists, compete in objective-based rounds.

Players must work together using communication, map knowledge, and mechanical skill to win rounds and matches.

The game includes different modes, but the most popular one is competitive, where teamwork and strategy are essential.
